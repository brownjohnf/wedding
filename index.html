Things to do in Seattle

There’s so much to do here, whether you’re looking for a big-city experience or want to get out and explore the abundance of nature.

In the City:

  • UW Arboretum
  • EMP Museum, Seattle Art Museum, Museum of Flight
  • Seattle Aquarium
  • Pike Place Market
  • The Fremont Troll (and bonus! Lenin)
  • Volunteer Park, Gasworks Park
  • Madison Park
  • Space Needle
